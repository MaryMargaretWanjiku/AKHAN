AKHAN NAMES

Generate Your Akhan Name Here!

The Akhan people of Ghana frequently name their children after the day of the week they were born and the order in which they were born

These "day names" have further meanings concerning the soul and the character of the person

Middle names have considerably more variety and can refer to their birth order, twin status, or an ancestor's middle name.

These names are also used among Ghanians living abroad and among Africans living in the diaspora who wish to identify with their ancenstral homeland

Most Ghanians have atleast one name from this system,even if they also have an Arabic or Western name.
